About "Daimond"

My first-ever attempt at animating in Photoshop. This piece was a foray into the expreience of exporting an animated file into a format that would be accepted by Instagram. It is, of course, the wrong size, so it needed to be cropped to fit the experience. It taught me a lot about the gif format, and how exactly I could render my ideas into it, while keeping it to a manageable size.
